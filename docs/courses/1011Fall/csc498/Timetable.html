CSC 498: Senior Project, Fall 2010

Timetable

Week 1 (8/27):
Course introduction, discuss project ideas. A one-paragraph description of one or two project possibilities is due by Monday, August 30. Email it to your instructor: bhoward@depauw.edu. Each description should be preceded with a tentative project title.
Week 2 (9/3):
Individual meetings as needed, time to be arranged. Your project proposal is due Friday, September 3 to allow time for an approval decision by meeting time next week.
Week 3 (9/10):
Required 10 minute individual meeting during regular class time. Meeting times will be assigned.
Week 4 (9/17):
Individual meetings as necessary based on any required proposal revisions.
Week 5 (9/24):
Checkpoint One presentation and S Presentation One.
Week 6 (10/1):
Individual meetings may be scheduled as needed.
Week 7 (10/8):
Individual meetings may be scheduled as needed.
Week 8 (10/15):
Checkpoint Two presentation and S Presentation Two.
Week 9 (10/29):
Ethics Exercise and S Discussion.
Week 10 (11/5):
Checkpoint Three presentation and S Presentation Three.
Week 11 (11/12):
Individual meetings may be scheduled as needed.
Week 12 (11/19):
Checkpoint Four (by appointment; meeting times will be assigned).
Week 13 (12/3):
Individual meetings may be scheduled as needed.
Week 14 (12/10):
Public Final Demos (S Presentation Four); Final project and report are due at this time.
