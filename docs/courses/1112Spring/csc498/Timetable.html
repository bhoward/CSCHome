CSC 498: Senior Project, Spring 2012

Timetable

Week 1 (1/30-2/3):
Course introduction, discuss project ideas. A one-paragraph description of one or two project possibilities is due by Monday, February 6. Email it to your instructor: bhoward@depauw.edu. Each description should be preceded with a tentative project title.
Week 2 (2/6-10):
Individual meetings as needed, time to be arranged. Your project proposal is due Friday, February 10 to allow time for an approval decision by meeting time next week.
Week 3 (2/13-17):
Required 10 minute individual meeting during regular class time. Meeting times will be assigned.
Week 4 (2/20-24):
Individual meetings as necessary based on any required proposal revisions.
Week 5 (2/27-3/2):
Individual meetings may be scheduled as needed.
Week 6 (3/5-9):
Checkpoint One presentation and S Presentation One.
Week 7 (3/12-16):
Individual meetings may be scheduled as needed.
Week 8 (3/19-23):
Checkpoint Two presentation and S Presentation Two.
Week 9 (4/2-6):
Ethics Exercise and S Discussion.
Week 10 (4/9-13):
Checkpoint Three presentation and S Presentation Three.
Week 11 (4/16-20):
Individual meetings may be scheduled as needed.
Week 12 (4/23-27):
Checkpoint Four (by appointment; meeting times will be assigned).
Week 13 (4/30-5/4):
Individual meetings may be scheduled as needed.
Week 14 (5/7-10):
Public Final Demos (S Presentation Four); Final project and report are due at this time.
