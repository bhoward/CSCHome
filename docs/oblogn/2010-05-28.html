Butler Butler Butler, the Time Paradox Butler

Butler Butler Butler was employed by the Professor, who had just invented a time machine. The butler borrowed the time machine to go back and cause his given name to be Butler; further back, he managed to change his family name to Butler as well. He did this because, paradoxically, the only reason the Professor gave him the job was because he was named Butler Butler, which seemed appropriate. This was just the start of the time paradoxes caused by B.B.B., who was only surpassed in annoying behavior by Willie DeVille, Evil Valet.
