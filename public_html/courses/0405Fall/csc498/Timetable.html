CSC 498 - Senior Project
Fall 2004-2005 Semester

Timetable

Week 1 (8/26):
Course introduction, discuss project ideas.
Week 2 (8/30-9/3):
Individual meetings as needed, time to be arranged. A one-paragraph description of one or two project possibilities is due by noon, Tuesday, Aug. 31. Email it to your instructor: michael.bell@acm.org, bhoward@depauw.edu, or aolowoye@cs.indiana.edu. Each description should be preceded with a tentative project title.
