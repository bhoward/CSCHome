CSC 498: Senior Project, Spring 2008

Timetable

Week 1 (1/28-2/1):
Course introduction, discuss project ideas. A one-paragraph description of one or two project possibilities is due by Friday, February 1. Email it to your instructor: bhoward@depauw.edu. Each description should be preceded with a tentative project title.
Week 2 (2/4-8):
Individual meetings as needed, time to be arranged. Your project proposal (hard copy) is due Friday, February 8 to allow time for an approval decision by meeting time next week.
Week 3 (2/11-15):
Required 15 minute individual meeting during regular class time. Meeting times will be assigned.
Week 4 (2/18-22):
Individual meetings as necessary based on any required proposal revisions.
Week 5 (2/25-29):
Checkpoint One presentation and S Presentation One.
Week 6 (3/3-7):
Individual meetings may be scheduled as needed.
Week 7 (3/10-14):
Individual meetings may be scheduled as needed.
Week 8 (3/17-21):
Checkpoint Two presentation and S Presentation Two.
Week 9 (3/31-4/4):
Ethics Exercise and `S' Discussion.
Week 10 (4/7-11):
Checkpoint Three presentation and S Presentation Three.
Week 11 (4/14-18):
Individual meetings may be scheduled as needed.
Week 12 (4/21-25):
Checkpoint Four (by appointment; meeting times will be assigned).
Week 13 (4/28-5/2):
Individual meetings may be scheduled as needed.
Week 14 (5/5-8):
Public Final Demos (S Presentation Four); Final project and report are due at this time.
